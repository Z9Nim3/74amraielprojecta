About BWS, Undri Boston World School is a co-ed school which is affiliated to Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE). It was established in 2018 and has an IGCSE board. It is a private school and is located in Pune, Maharashtra. It is one of the top schools in Pune and is managed by RIMS International. The school offers admission from Nursery to Class 12 (along with A level course) to the students. It provides extra and co-curricular activities to the students like military training, dance classes, animation activities, developing their hobbies in scientific activities etc. Some of the facilities provided at Boston World school are smart classrooms, music and dance rooms, library and reading rooms, outdoor sports, medical room and transportation.
